Fast Compatibility Checklist

  • CPU socket matches motherboard; board supports your CPU gen.
  • RAM type (DDR4/DDR5) and rated speed supported by motherboard.
  • Case fits motherboard size, GPU length, and cooler/radiator height.
  • PSU has enough wattage and the right connectors (EPS/PCIe/12VHPWR).
  • Storage slots available (M.2 count/PCIe lanes) for your SSD plans.
  • Monitor resolution/refresh pairs well with your GPU.
