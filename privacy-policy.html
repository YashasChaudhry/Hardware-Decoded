Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 16, 2025

At Hardware Decoded, we respect your privacy. This policy explains what we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

1) Information We Collect
  • Newsletter data: your email address when you subscribe.
  • Basic analytics: aggregated, non-identifying data such as page views and device type.
2) How We Use Information
  • To send you updates when you subscribe.
  • To improve content and site performance using aggregated analytics.
3) Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may use privacy-friendly analytics tools. Service providers (e.g., email delivery) only process data on our behalf and under confidentiality obligations.

4) Cookies & Tracking

We keep tracking minimal. If we use cookies, it is primarily for basic, aggregated analytics and improving user experience. You can adjust cookie preferences in your browser settings.

5) Data Retention

We keep your email address only while you remain subscribed or as needed to provide the service. You can unsubscribe any time—your email will be removed from our mailing list.

6) Your Rights
  • Access, update, or delete your data.
  • Unsubscribe from emails via the link in any message.
  • Contact us for any privacy request.
7) Children’s Privacy

Hardware Decoded is intended for a general audience and not directed to children under 13.

8) Contact

Questions about this policy? Email us at hardwaredecoded@gmail.com.
