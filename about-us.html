About Hardware Decoded

Demystifying PC hardware, one component at a time

Hi, I'm Yashas Chaudhry

Welcome to Hardware Decoded – your gateway to understanding the fascinating world of PC components and hardware.

I created this blog because of my deep love for technology, computers, and hardware in general. When I started my journey into PC building, I couldn't find any blogs that focused solely on PC components – explaining how each part matters, how they work together, and most importantly, making it accessible for beginners.

It took me a long time to understand the importance of different PC parts when I first started. I want to make sure you don't have to go through that same struggle. My goal is simple: make it easy for people to understand the world of computer hardware.

My Mission

To help beginners start their journey into custom PC building and hardware by providing clear, easy-to-understand content that breaks down complex concepts into digestible pieces.

💡
Simplify Complex Topics

I break down complicated hardware concepts into easy-to-understand explanations that anyone can follow, regardless of their technical background.

👥
Focus on Beginners

Every article is written with newcomers in mind. I remember being confused when I started, and I don't want anyone else to feel that way.

🖥️
PC Components Only

Unlike other tech blogs that cover everything, I focus exclusively on PC hardware – CPUs, GPUs, motherboards, RAM, storage, and more.

My Tech Journey

7th Grade
The Spark Ignites

My interest in technology began in 7th grade when I started following Apple and Google events online. I was fascinated by how these companies pushed the boundaries of what technology could do.

8th Grade
Discovering PC Building

In 8th grade, I stumbled upon the world of PC building and was immediately hooked. The idea that you could choose every component and build something truly custom was mind-blowing to me.

The Dream
Blog Idea Born

I wanted to start this blog back then, but I didn't know how to make a website. The idea stayed with me through the years as I continued learning about hardware.

Today
Dream Realized

Now, this blog is my way to stay connected to tech while helping others discover the amazing world of PC hardware. It's the realization of a dream years in the making.
